About

I am a software developer turned technical writer with a background in web, metaverse, and GIS technologies.

I take pride in being able to navigate that kind of professional environment to offer simple and effective solutions for complex problems. I now use those skills to serve the private sector, providing the same level of expertise to a broader market.
